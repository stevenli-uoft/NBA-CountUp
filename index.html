The 3-Point Revolution

The NBA has undergone significant changes over time, with one of the most dramatic shifts being the rise of the three-point shot. This visualization explores how the game has evolved from a predominantly inside scoring approach to a perimeter-focused style.

The Three-Point Revolution

Since its introduction in the 1979-80 season, the three-point shot has transformed from a rarely-used tactic to the centerpiece of modern NBA offenses.
